1Overview

Dralvo GoldWave is an XAUUSD (Gold) scalping EA for MetaTrader 5, running on real ticks. The idea: a recovery (reverse-martingale) strategy makes money when the market ranges and dies in a strong trend — so GoldWave only enters when it detects a SIDEWAY market on M1.

  • Symbol/Timeframe: XAUUSD, signals computed on M1.
  • Type: Sideway scalp + SAR-martingale (one position at a time).
  • Shields: step cap (caps the tail) + equity-guard (hard brake).

2Entry mechanism

  1. Detect sideway (M1): ADX < threshold (no trend) + price contained in an N-candle range. Outside a sideway → NO entry.
  2. Mean-reversion at the edges: price touches the range bottom → BUY, touches the top → SELL. Lot = base.
  3. Recovery (SAR-martingale): if SL is hit → reverse direction + scale the lot (×2.0 or ×2.75) and re-enter immediately. Repeat until a win.
  4. Win → reset lot back to base, wait for a new sideway signal.
  5. Hitting the step cap → CUT the chain (take the loss, reset) — caps the tail risk.
Why NO trailing / trend-cut? Backtested: they break the recovery math of the martingale (a winning trade must reach its full TP to recover the chain) → they worsen results. Proper capital protection = step cap + equity-guard + enough capital.

Attaching the EA

  1. Copy Dralvo_GoldWave.ex5 into MQL5\Experts, refresh the Navigator.
  2. Open an XAUUSD chart (any timeframe — the signal is computed internally on M1).
  3. Drag the EA onto the chart → Inputs → Load → choose the setfile matching your capital.
  4. Enable Algo Trading.

Activate the License (REQUIRED on demo/live)

5Risk & notes

  • It is a martingale: the equity curve looks great most of the time, but one losing chain beyond the cap = a big loss / blown account. Cap + equity-guard mitigate, they don't eliminate.
  • Tail lot grows exponentially: the higher the multiplier, the more brutal the lot at deep steps (HighProfit step 10 = 90 lots). Capital MUST absorb it.
  • Trades only in sideways, so there are periods where the EA "sits still" waiting for a range — normal, not a bug.
  • Low-spread broker for Gold; backtested over 34 months, but future markets may differ.

⚠️ Past backtests do NOT guarantee the future. GoldWave is a martingale EA — it carries blow-up risk when a strong trend runs beyond the cap. Capital must cover the lot at the tail step.
